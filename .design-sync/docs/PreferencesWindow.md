The full Preferences window (720px) with sidebar navigation across General, Recording, Prompt, Pipeline, Integrations, Permissions, and Advanced panes.
No props; pane selection is internal state (General opens first). Use it whole for settings mockups, or compose new panes from PWGroup / PWRow / PWToggle / PWField / PWSegmented / PWSlider and friends to match its look.
